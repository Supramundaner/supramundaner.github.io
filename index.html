About Me

Hi, I am Yuzhuo Ao (敖宇卓), a third-year BSc student in Data Science and Technology +AI at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). Currently I am an exchange student at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). My research interests are centered on machine learning and computer vision, with a particular focus on systems for real-world modeling, representation and interaction.

I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Chi-Keung Tang and Prof. Yu-Wing Tai on computer vision. I am also fortunate to collaborate with Prof. Shangzhe Wu on controllable 3D generation.

Feel free to reach out for potential collaborations or discussions!
