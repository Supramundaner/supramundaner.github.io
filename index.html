About Me

Hi, I am AO Yuzhuo (敖宇卓), a third-year BSc student in Data Science and Technology +AI at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). Currently I am an exchange student at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL)!

My research interests are centered on machine learning and computer vision, with a particular focus on modeling and human-like understanding of the real world. My most recent work has been concentrated on 3D vision and 3D generation. I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Chi-Keung Tang and Prof. Yu-Wing Tai on computer vision. I am also fortunate to collaborate with Prof. Shangzhe Wu on controllable 3D generation!

Feel free to reach out for potential collaborations or discussions!
